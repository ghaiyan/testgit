>> About this database
A brief introduction:
The REs and RE/TM Compounds Database (the rare-earth metals and rare-earth/transition-metal compounds database) was created and constructed by Prof. Xiaoyan Song's research group of Metallic Nanomaterials and Computational Material Science at Beijing University of Technology, P. R. China (URL: http://cms.bjut.edu.cn).

The REs and RE/TM Compounds Database consists of four gradual menus, i.e. the ‘Log-in’ interface, the ‘Main Menu’ interface, the ‘Data’ interface, and the ‘Source of Data’ interface.

  • The 'Log in' interface
    • is used to verify the users ID.
  • The 'Main Menu' interface
    • is used to choose the material for viewing the data of structure and properties.
  • The 'Data' interface
    • is used to view the data of structure and properties at the room temperature and at high temperatures.
  • The 'Source of Data' interface
    • is used to view the detailed information of data. There is a button 'Detailed information of data' at the right lower corner of each main 'Data' interface. Click this button to view the source of the data, the detailed information of preparation of the samples (nanocrystalline rare-earth metals and rare-earth/ transition-metal compounds), and the measuring methods of the properties.
    The link to this database is via "Metals and Alloys" Database through NIMS (National Institute for Material Science, Japan) Materials Database.
